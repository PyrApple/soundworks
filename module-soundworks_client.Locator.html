Class: Locator

soundworks/client.Locator

Interface of the client 'locator' service.

This service is one of the provided services aimed at identifying clients inside the experience along with the 'placer' and 'checkin' services.

The 'locator' service allows a client to give its approximate location inside a graphical representation of the area as defined in the server's setup configuration entry.

The service must be used with its server-side counterpart
